Unhurry

A calmer way through your day — one step at a time.

Unhurry is a visual routine planner for people who lose track of time. Build your morning once, then live it one step at a time with timers you can see from across the room. No subscription. No account. No AI. Works completely offline.

Privacy Policy

The full policy is also on its own page: privacy.html.

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Unhurry collects no data. None. There is no account, no sign-in, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

  • Your routines, schedules, and settings are stored only on your device. We cannot see them.
  • Unhurry has no servers and makes no network connections. It works fully offline.
  • If you turn on the optional calendar view, Unhurry reads your calendar events on your device only to show them beside your routines. Those events are never transmitted or stored anywhere by us.
  • Purchases are handled entirely by Apple. We never see your payment information.

Because we collect nothing, there is nothing for us to share, sell, or lose. If you delete the app, your on-device data is removed with it.

This policy may be updated as the app evolves; material changes will be noted here.
